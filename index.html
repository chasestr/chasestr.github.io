Sour Cream Chicken Enchiladas

  • Ingredients
  • Equipment
  • Instructions

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Chicken Breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 1/2 Onion, chopped
  • 1 Can of Rotel
  • 1 tbsp (or half packet) of Taco Seasoning
  • 1 Can Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 8 oz Sour Cream
  • Handful of Shredded Cheese (any kind)
  • 8-10 Tortillas

Equipment Needed

  • 9x13 Baking Pan
  • Large Skillet

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In skillet, brown the chopped onions until soft.
  3. Add in the shredded chicken, taco seasoning, Rotel, and half the can of cream of chicken soup.
  4. Cook over medium high heat until mixed thoroughly and heated through.
  5. Spoon the chicken mixture evenly into the tortillas.
  6. Roll and place in a 9x13 baking dish with the seam side down. If the tortillas need to be more pliable, microwave them for 30 seconds.
  7. Continue previous two steps until your pan is full.
  8. Mix the remaining cream of chicken soup, sour cream, and milk in a bowl until combined. If the sauce is too thick, add a small amount of chicken broth to thin it out.
  9. Pour sour cream mixture over your rolled tortillas. Sprinkle with the shredded cheese on top.
  10. Bake for 20 minutes until heated through.
  11. Serve!

Enjoy!
